    W1 Backup Options

    https://www.howtogeek.com/427480/how-to-back-up-your-linux-system/

    I had a corrupted motherboard. It could not hold the Windows OS (think blue screen of death). I scrapped that for Ubuntu, and it crashed about 8 times in the span of one month. It was the stable build. Now that I have a new machine, I want to not have to continue to download all the configurations I made. I was not able to perform it via rsync but I did use grsync.
